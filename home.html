Why Financial Literacy Matters?

Financial literacy helps in budgeting by ensuring that you spend wisely and save effectively. It prevents overspending and allows better financial planning. It also encourages savings and investments, helping you grow your wealth over time. Managing debt is another important aspect. Many people struggle with loans and credit card debt. Learning about financial literacy helps in avoiding debt traps and making responsible financial choices.
